How to Play Game

  • Click the play Game button on the navigation menu.
  • Hit click to play guiz to start quiz.
  • This quiz contains five questions.
  • By clicking on the profile button you can choose username
  • Answer the question by selecting one of the four choices
  • An alert box will appear to display the correct or wrong answer.
  • At the end of quiz it will show you the score and play again button to restart quiz.
